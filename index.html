kodaikanal

i had visited in May 2014

Kodaikanal is one of my favourite spot of tour. We have gone with family, friends etc many times. A superb place to hang out with friends while enjoying spicy road snacks and the nature. A peaceful place for cycling and boating.



goa

i had visited in April 2019

Goa is one of the most beautiful places in India situated in the western coast of Arabian Sea. Catching up the sunset in the scenic beaches, holding up the hands of your loved one, taking a long relaxed walk on the sands and of course, not to forget about indulging in some rich sea food, it is a pure bliss! What more can you ask for on a holiday? and I can't forget 1 time my brother told us that we should jump in the water when we are riding a boat in the middle of the sea!

review on the movies

Avengers Endgame
Did not like it...

Fan service doesn't make good movie, plot does. And by that measure, Endgame is the worst MCU movie ever made.

There are many reasons why Endgame is a huge mess, and the main reason is, the plot makes no sense at all.

The Lion King
Awwsome !

Every single part of The Lion King is alright. It's just masterful filmmaking, which is made all the more impressive when you remember that it's a cartoon from 1994. The way Scar stalks around watching Mufasa try to rescue Simba. ... I wonder how much time Scar spent thinking of that.

Frozen 2
Beautiful, Enchanting and Layered

In an overall sense, Frozen 2 has a better storyline than its predecessor. It deals with some heavier themes, has different consequences, it's just stronger overall. Frozen 2 goes deeper, and digs into Elsa and Anna's background in some new ways.
